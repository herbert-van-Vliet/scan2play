Enable NFC

  1. Enable NFC on your device
    Go to Settings → Connections → NFC
  2. Try Chrome
    Web NFC may not work in your browser. You could test with Chrome.
  3. Grant NFC permission
    Go to Settings → Apps → [Your Browser] → Permissions → NFC
